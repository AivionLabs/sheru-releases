How it works

Local reads. Anonymised AI.

Step 1

🖥️

Reads locally

Sheru reads your mail, calendar, files, and system metrics directly on your device. Nothing is sent anywhere at this stage. Everything runs on your machine.

Step 2

🛡️

Strips your data

Before any AI call, Sheru replaces names, email addresses, phone numbers, and amounts with placeholders. You see exactly what was removed — and approve before it goes.

Step 3

✨

AI reasons

Only the anonymised text reaches Gemini via the AivionLabs proxy (zero-log, no-retention). The result comes back and Sheru restores your real names locally.

Capabilities

Everything in one place

From finding a file to drafting a reply to checking your calendar — all from a single chat window, all on your machine.

📬

Mail — read, rank, draft

Reads Outlook, Apple Mail, Gmail, and any IMAP account locally. Ranks by importance for your role. Drafts replies with AI after stripping identifiers.

📅

Calendar awareness

Knows your schedule from Outlook, macOS Calendar, or Google Calendar. Gets a 5-minute meeting prep brief before every call — attendee emails, related files, last notes.

🔍

File search & open

"Find my offer letter" returns the path in under a second. Drag any document onto Sheru and get an instant AI summary — PDF, Word, spreadsheet.

🌅

Morning briefing

First thing every morning: today's interviews, pending approvals, urgent emails, and calendar — all in one card. Role-tailored. No AI tokens required for the daily summary.

🖥️

System monitoring

CPU, RAM, disk, network, sensors, and processes — always available. "Why is my PC slow?" gets a real answer with the actual process name, not a generic shrug.

🧠

Memory

Say "Sheru, remember my manager is Sarah" and it sticks. Inspect and delete everything from the "What I remember" panel. No database jargon — plain English cards.

📡

Network awareness

Discovers your router, LAN devices, and active connections via local ARP scanning. Everything stays on your network — nothing is forwarded.

🔌

MCP support

Connect to external MCP servers (Slack, Notion, Jira). Or let Sheru expose itself as an MCP server — Claude Desktop and Cursor can reach your files and calendar through it.

🎙️

Voice input

Optional faster-whisper integration for hands-free use. Runs locally on Apple Silicon or CUDA — no cloud transcription.

Privacy

You see exactly
what was removed
before it goes.

Every AI call strips your data of personal identifiers first. You get a confirmation showing the substitutions — and you can cancel if anything looks wrong. No raw personal data ever reaches AivionLabs or Google.

  • ✓
    Names, emails, amounts replaced with [PERSON_1], [EMAIL_1], [AMOUNT_1] before leaving your machine.
  • ✓
    Gemini uses Google's no-retention API tier. Your data is not used for training.
  • ✓
    AivionLabs proxy is zero-log. It forwards requests and deducts tokens — stores nothing else.
  • ✓
    OAuth tokens in your system keychain. Gmail and Google Calendar credentials never touch AivionLabs servers.
  • ✓
    Full erasure: rm -rf ~/.sheru — or click "Forget everything" in the app.
  • ✓
    GDPR: AivionLabs receives no personal data. You are the sole controller of data on your device.
