Capabilities

Everything runs on your machine

Ask questions in plain English. Sheru reads real data from your system and gives structured answers — no internet required.

🖥️

System monitoring

CPU, memory, disk, network, temperature sensors, and running processes — all at a glance or on demand.

📡

Network awareness

Discover your router, connected devices, and active connections via local ARP scanning — nothing leaves your LAN.

📬

Local mail

Read and summarise emails from Outlook, Apple Mail, Gmail, or any IMAP account — processed entirely on-device.

📅

Calendar

Query and reason about events from Outlook, macOS Calendar, or Google Calendar without leaving your machine.

🔌

MCP support

Connect to external MCP servers for additional tools, or expose Sheru itself as an MCP server for other agents.

🎙️

Voice input

Optional faster-whisper integration for hands-free operation on Apple Silicon and CUDA-capable machines.

Privacy first

Your data
never leaves
your machine.

  • No telemetry. Sheru collects nothing. No analytics, no crash reports, no usage data.
  • No accounts. No registration. No login. No subscription tracking.
  • Mail stays local. Email content is read and processed entirely on your device.
  • OAuth tokens in your keychain. Gmail and Google Calendar tokens are stored in your system keychain — never on AivionLabs servers.
  • Audit log on-device. Every action is logged to ~/.sheru/logs/ for your review only.
  • GDPR compliant. Because no personal data reaches us, you have full control. Delete everything by removing ~/.sheru.
