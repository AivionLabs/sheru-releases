## v0.0.1

First beta release.

**What's included:**
- Email: summarise inbox, rank today's unread emails by importance (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail)
- Calendar: see today's meetings (macOS EventKit, Outlook, AppleScript fallback)
- Files: search, open, and AI-summarise documents (PDFs, text)
- Memory: remember and recall facts about people and context
- System diagnostics: CPU, RAM, disk, battery, top processes, network devices
- Morning briefing: daily digest of calendar + email tailored to your role
- Gemini integration with PII scrubbing before any data leaves your machine
- 11 role profiles: Manager, Developer, HR, Sales, Support, Ops, Legal, Marketing, Accounting, Admin, Executive
- MCP server: expose Sheru tools to other agents
- Tauri desktop app with system tray and in-app auto-updater
